OH, Canada!

Canada, a wide country known for foods you’d find in the atlantic and the pacific provinces, for creating staple hearty foods in the prairies and others from North America.Having quite the food palette you can easily find yourself all sorts of food that's to your liking, as the foods of Canada is quite a smorgasbord.

If you have your eye on some good seafood then the Maritimes is where you want to be; New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, Prince Edward Island, Newfoundland & Labrador. Which brings the kind of seafood its best known for:mussels, lobsters, cod,smoked salmon, oysters

If you are the person who enjoys eating meat then Alberta is the province for you:nicknamed as the “cattle capital of Canada”. Alberta has made a name in cooking the best beef you can get, Alberta beef.Whether beef has been cooked done in a barbecue, braised, salted, grilled, minced, skewered you name it!
