French cuisine is renouned for many different dishes many of which sound pretty interesting to say the least.. frogs legs and snails; yum! but everyones favourite has to be the french patisseries. These consist of pastries and sweet treats and cakes, all focused on being well presented of a good quality.

In england, a patisserie is equivalent to a bakery in which we can find some similar items like bread, croissonts and pain au chocolats.
